introduction

WELCOMEE TO MY SPACE! I'm Othmane Oublal, a passionate sound engineer and multimedia enthusiast based in Marrakech. With a solid foundation in recording and post-production gained from my studies at the Institute of Superior Audiovisual and Cinema Professions, I've had the privilege of contributing my skills to various projects, including serving as an operator for the short film "Mr Alligator" and acting as a sound engineer for diverse events. My proficiency extends to music mixing, decision-making, and creative problem-solving. As a versatile professional, I've ventured into freelancing, collaborating as an F.O.H engineer at Show Partner. Fluent in Arabic, English, and French, I am not just technically adept but also deeply connected to the global language of creativity. Join me on a journey where technology meets artistry, and let's create captivating sonic experiences together.

Mr Alligator

I worked as a sound operator for the short film "Mr Alligator." Mr. Alligator is a young crocodile living among humans, and in this film, we explore how he spends his day and copes with different characteristics and problems. Initially known as Saad, this alligator underwent an identity crisis and created the character of Mr. Alligator as a way to rebel and be free. This transformation to a free spirit has contributed to a healthy lifestyle. With the help of a group of young filmmakers, we witness how he lives as a civilian, sharing a part of his life with the crew shooting a documentary about him.

DOCUMENTARY "THE FORGOTTEN TANAGHMELT"

THIS PROJECT WAS MY CHILD DREAM , I AM SO PROUD TO GIVE LIFE TO THIS PROJECT ABOUT TANAGHMELT THAT I HAVE BEEN HEARING STORIES WHEN GROWING UP FROM MY FAMILY , Hidden between the Atlas Mountains, ZAOUIAT TANAGHMEL is full of Moroccan historical testimonies. 9 km from Ouzoud, this small village built around a hill survives thanks to water sources and agricultural operations. 100 Amazigh families live there and help each other to preserve their seasonal customs and festivities. A man works every day to save this spiritual place from the problems of the village. A village impacted by the exodus of people who leave it to settle in the city, a village cracked by time and left abandoned. It is the story of the leader of ZAOUIAT, a man who swore to protect his heritage, but it is also the story of a region frozen in time.
